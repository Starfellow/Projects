About AFC

The Association of Female Comrades (AFC) is a dynamic NGO of young women united to empower, protect, and amplify female voices across Nigeria. We are passionate about ending femicide, gender-based violence, and ritual killings, while building a society where women thrive in safety and dignity.

  • Empower women with skills, knowledge & opportunities
  • Advocate & protect against gender-based violence and abuse
  • Promote inclusivity through collaboration and partnerships
  • Build capacity with training and mentorship
  • Sensitize communities with national campaigns like Voices Against Silence
